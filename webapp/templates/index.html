Face Detection Using CNN

Face detection is a computer application used to detect the presence of human faces in images and video sequences.Face detection software can identify a person or a particular face in an image, thus helping the owner can detect presence of friends and family members in images or videos.

This tech keeps overgrowing day by day. Although it can be used for various purposes, the portion of its applications that results from biometric authentication perspective is enormous.We can use this tech on our phones to unlock the screen, make payments, login to services and websites, detect emotion or even identify someone.

Facerec is a product that helps recognizing the face and have a wide domain of use. The product is based on a deep learning model that is trained on a large dataset of finite faces. For the recognition the model uses :

  • The Haar Cascade
  • Convolutional Neural Networks(CNN)
